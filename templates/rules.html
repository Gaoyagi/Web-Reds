  1. The goal of the game is to get the lowest scoring hand possible
  2. Every player hand has 4 cards, set up in a 2x2 grid in front of them
  3. Players can only look at the 2 cards closest to them ONCE
  4. Card Values:
    1. >numbers are worth their numerical value
    2. >Jokers are worth 0
    3. >Aces are worth 1
    4. >Face cards are worth 10
    5. >Exception to that is Red Kings(king of hearts and diamonds) which are worth -2
  5. Every Turn a player has 1 action of a possible 3 actions:
    1. Draw a card from deck and exchange it with one of their cards if they want to or discard it face up
    2. Take the top card of the discard pile and exchange it with one of their cards
    3. Declare: means you think you have lowest hand, your cards are locked in and everyone takes one more turn before comparing cards
